# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We commit to a respectful, inclusive, and student-friendly collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and constructive language.
- Respect differing skill levels and backgrounds.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Keep project discussions relevant and professional.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, insults, discrimination, doxxing, or deliberate disruption are not acceptable in this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when conduct harms the project or contributors.
